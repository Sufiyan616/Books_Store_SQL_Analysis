PROJECT OBJECTIVES
- Understand book sales data
- Find top-selling books and genres
- Analyze customer buying behavior
- Check stock and inventory status
- Practice real-world SQL queries

KEY ANALYSIS PERFORMED
- Found books published after a specific year
- Checked expensive books with low stock
- Identified customers from specific countries
- Analyzed orders from a particular month
- Found top-selling genres and authors
- Identified customers with multiple orders
- Found the most frequently ordered books

KEY INSIGHTS
- Fantasy genre has high demand
- Some authors contribute more to total sales
- A small group of customers places multiple orders
- Expensive books usually have limited stock
- Sales analysis helps understand customer trends
